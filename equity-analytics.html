NIFTY 50: Predictive Equity & Risk Engine

This interactive dashboard showcases a Bayesian time-series forecasting model (Prophet) developed on Zorin OS[cite: 3]. The system parallel-processes NIFTY 50 ticker data via a Python ETL pipeline, stores results in PostgreSQL, and visualizes 90-day price trajectories with shaded uncertainty bands[cite: 3].
